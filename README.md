# Educational Information

## Electrical Safety Tips

- Never overload outlets
- Replace damaged electrical cords
- Turn off power before repairs
- Use certified electricians
- Schedule yearly inspections

---

## Plumbing Safety Tips

- Repair leaks immediately
- Avoid pouring grease into drains
- Check pipes for corrosion
- Install water-saving fixtures
- Know the location of your main water valve
